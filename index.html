About me

Software Engineer with a background in culinary industry and applied sciences, I attended UW-Whitewater for Biology and Physics Education. In a restaurant there are a number of moving parts, not unlike code. With software, it's managing multiple pages; yet both have a common goal that everybody works towards. I love it when ingredients and components of a dish fall into place, similarly to how code fits together when its all functioning; the similarities continue when it comes to the amount of creativity you have while cooking/coding. The techniques you implement determine the outcome of the product. Coding pushes me out of my comfort zone in ways I never imagined, and that's what keeps me coming back.
